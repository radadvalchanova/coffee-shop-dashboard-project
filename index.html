Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

A clean, interactive Excel dashboard exploring sales performance across three coffee shop locations (January–June 2023). The focus is simple: make patterns easy to spot and decisions easier to make.

Excel PivotTables PivotCharts Slicers Sales Analysis

Dashboard preview

This view reflects the slicer set to All locations. Use the pills above to switch location-specific versions.

What’s inside

  • Revenue trend (Jan–Jun) to spot growth and seasonality.
  • Transactions by weekday to understand weekly rhythm.
  • Transactions by hour to identify peak staffing windows.
  • Product category performance to see what drives volume.
  • Top product types (table) for quick priorities and opportunity spotting.

Note: The live slicer interaction exists in Excel. On the web page, I’m presenting the same dashboard in four curated snapshots — one for each slicer state — so viewers can still explore the story without downloading anything first.
